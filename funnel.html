Walk through each stage, from the first click to the coaching offer, and see exactly what gets delivered at every step.

Step 1

Traffic hits a landing page with a quiz

Paid or organic traffic lands on a page built around one question: who is this lead, really?

Instead of a generic opt-in form, they're walked through a short quiz that collects the data needed to serve them something specific, not something broadcast to everyone.

Step 2

Answers determine the segment

The primary signal is kids' age range. Each group has different psychology, schedule constraints, and parenting pressures, so each gets a completely different funnel.

Secondary answers (challenge type, consistency level, main goal) further personalize the copy within the segment.

Step 3

Each segment gets a matched lead magnet

No two segments receive the same guide. Each lead magnet is written for the exact situation of that segment: different title, different framing, different content.

The delivery email also references their quiz answers, so the experience feels personal from the very first message they open.
