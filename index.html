"What if my offer only targets one type of audience?"

Most funnels don't fail because they lack segmentation.
They fail because they lack any layer of personalization beyond a first name.

Even if your offer targets one specific niche, there's still a lot we can do:

  • Behavior-based sequences that shift based on what each lead clicks, opens, or ignores

  • Conversion paths built around intent signals, not arbitrary drip schedules

Segmentation is one tool. Personalization is the whole toolbox.
